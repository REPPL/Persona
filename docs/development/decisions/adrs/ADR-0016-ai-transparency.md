# ADR-0016: AI Transparency Commitment

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Persona is developed with significant AI assistance. We have options:
- No disclosure
- Minimal disclosure
- Full transparency

## Decision

Full AI transparency:
- All AI-assisted commits attributed
- Thinking logs for significant decisions
- Retrospectives document AI reasoning
- Architecture decisions capture AI input

## Consequences

**Positive:**
- Honest about development process
- Educational for others
- Reasoning preserved for future
- Trust through transparency

**Negative:**
- More documentation effort
- May raise skepticism about quality
- Commits have longer messages
